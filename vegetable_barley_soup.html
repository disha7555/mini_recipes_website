Vegetable & barley soup

serves 4 | prep 10 mins | cooking 50 mins
1 tbs olive oil
1 brown onion, chopped
1 large carrot, peeled, halved
lengthways, chopped
4 middle bacon rashers, trimmed, chopped
2 celery stalks, leaves reserved, stalks
chopped
400g can whole peeled tomatoes
1 large zucchini, halved lengthways,
chopped
1 tbs chicken stock powder
1.5L (6 cups) cold water
110g (1‚ÅÑ2 cup) pearl barley, rinsed
1 Heat oil in a large saucepan over
medium heat. Add onion, carrot, bacon
and chopped celery. Cook for 10 minutes
or until onion softens. Add tomato, zucchini,
stock powder and water. Bring to the boil.
2 Add the barley. Reduce heat to low.
Simmer, partially covered, for 40 minutes
or until barley is tender. Chop reserved
celery leaves. Stir into soup.
